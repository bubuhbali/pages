Bubuh Bali Pak Item

September 6, 2020

Bubuh Bali Pak Item is one of the cool spot in Ubud. It is located within rice field but the remain accessible from the main street. 

There are some beautiful attractions around Bubuh Bali Pak Item that you can combine if you visit this area. 

  1. Gunung Kawi Sebatu Temple (2 minutes drive)
  2. Tirta Empul Holy Spring Water Temple (7 minutes drive)
  3. Kintamani Lake View
